A few notable mushrooms that are potentially deadly upon consumption
Be extremely careful with these!
- Deathcap (Amanita phalloides)
- Conocybe filaris
- Webcaps (Cortinarius species)
- Autumn Skullcap (Galerina marginata)
- Destroying Angels (Amanita species)
- Podostroma cornu-damae
- Deadly Dapperling (Lepiota brunneoincaranta)
